• Take one capsule at breakfast time, one capsule at lunch time and one capsule at dinner time.
  • Avoid food containing fat between meals.
  • Tell your doctor if you miss a main meal or have a meal with no fat.
  • If you take more capsules than prescribed, contact a doctor or pharmacist.
  • If you forget to take Xenical, take it as soon as you remember within one hour of your last meal.
  • Do not change the prescribed dose unless told by your doctor.
  • Report any side effects to your doctor or pharmacist.
  • Store Xenical in its original package and keep the blister in the outer carton to protect from light and moisture.
