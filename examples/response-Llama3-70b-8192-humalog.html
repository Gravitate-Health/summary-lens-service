What Humalog Mix50 is and what it is used for

  • Humalog Mix50 is used to treat diabetes
  • It works more quickly than normal human insulin and lasts a shorter time than soluble insulin (2 to 5 hours)
  • It is suitable for use in adults and children

What you need to know before you take Humalog Mix50

  • Do not use Humalog Mix50 if you think hypoglycaemia (low blood sugar) is starting
  • Check the pack and cartridge label for the name and type of insulin
  • Be careful when changing insulin types or doses
  • Inform your doctor if you have recently become ill, have trouble with your kidneys or liver, or are exercising more than usual
  • Inform your doctor if you are planning to go abroad or have recently changed your insulin therapy

How to take Humalog Mix50

  • Inject Humalog Mix50 within 15 minutes of a meal
  • Rotate the injection site to prevent skin changes
  • Use the correct injection technique and dose as advised by your doctor
  • Check your blood sugar levels regularly

Possible side effects

  • Systemic allergy (rash, blood pressure drop, difficulty breathing, heart beating fast, wheezing, sweating)
  • Local allergy (redness, swelling, or itching around the injection site)
  • Lipodystrophy (lumps under the skin)
  • Oedema (swelling in arms, ankles; fluid retention)
  • Hypoglycaemia (low blood sugar) symptoms: tiredness, rapid heartbeat, nervousness, feeling sick, headache, cold sweat
  • Hyperglycaemia (high blood sugar) symptoms: feeling sleepy, no appetite, flushed face, fruity smell on the breath, thirst, feeling or being sick

How to store Humalog Mix50

  • Store in a refrigerator (2°C – 8°C) before first use
  • Keep at room temperature (below 30°C) and discard after 28 days
  • Do not freeze, keep near heat, or in the sun
  • Keep out of the sight and reach of children
