• What Humalog Mix50 is and what it is used for: Humalog Mix50 is used to treat diabetes, working more quickly than normal human insulin to control glucose levels in the blood.
  • Key characteristics: Humalog Mix50 is a substitute for the body's own insulin, works quickly and lasts a shorter time than soluble insulin (2 to 5 hours), and is suitable for use in adults and children.
  • What you need to know before taking Humalog Mix50: Do not use Humalog Mix50 if you think you have low blood sugar, are allergic to insulin lispro or other ingredients, or have recently become ill, have trouble with your kidneys or liver, or are exercising more than usual.
  • How to take Humalog Mix50: Inject Humalog Mix50 within 15 minutes of a meal, under the skin, and not intravenously; rotate the injection site to prevent skin changes.
  • Possible side effects: Systemic allergy, local allergy, lipodystrophy, oedema, hypoglycaemia, and hyperglycaemia; report any side effects to your doctor or pharmacist.
  • Common problems of diabetes: Hypoglycaemia (low blood sugar), hyperglycaemia (high blood sugar), and diabetic ketoacidosis; recognize warning symptoms and seek medical help immediately if severe.
  • How to store Humalog Mix50: Store in a refrigerator before first use, keep in use at room temperature, and discard after 28 days; do not freeze, put near heat or in the sun, or keep in the fridge.
  • Important safety information: Do not change your insulin unless your doctor tells you to, be careful when driving or operating machinery if you have hypoglycaemia, and always carry something to show you are diabetic and sugar with you.
