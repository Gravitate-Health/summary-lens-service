• Take Karvea until your doctor tells you otherwise.

  • Patients with high blood pressure: usual dose is 150 mg once a day (two tablets a day).

  • Patients with high blood pressure and type 2 diabetes with kidney disease: preferred maintenance dose is 300 mg (four tablets a day) once daily.

  • If you take more Karvea than you should, contact your doctor immediately.

  • If you forget to take Karvea, just take the next dose as normal. Do not take a double dose to make up for a forgotten dose.

  • Possible side effects include allergic skin reactions, localized swelling of the face, lips, and/or tongue, dizziness, feeling sick/vomiting, fatigue, blood tests may show raised levels of potassium, creatine kinase enzyme, and haemoglobin.

  • Other possible side effects include heart rate increased, flushing, cough, diarrhoea, indigestion/heartburn, sexual dysfunction, chest pain, and decreased number of red blood cells.

  • Report any side effects to your doctor or pharmacist. You can also report side effects directly via the national reporting system.
