• Take Karvea with a sufficient amount of fluid (e.g., one glass of water).
  • You can take it with or without food.
  • Try to take your daily dose at about the same time each day.
  • Continue taking Karvea until your doctor tells you otherwise.
  • The usual dose for high blood pressure is 150 mg once a day (two tablets a day).
  • The dose may be increased to 300 mg (four tablets a day) once daily depending on blood pressure response.
  • For patients with high blood pressure and type 2 diabetes with kidney disease, the preferred maintenance dose is 300 mg (four tablets a day) once daily.
  • If you accidentally take too many tablets, contact your doctor immediately.
  • If you forget to take Karvea, just take the next dose as normal. Do not take a double dose to make up for a forgotten dose.
  • Report any side effects to your doctor or pharmacist.
  • Keep this medicine out of the sight and reach of children.
